Welcome to Chop Life
Chop Life stands out amongst other African restaurants. Our commitment to bringing the perfect mix of traditional and modern flavors is unrivaled by any other restaurant. From our extensive menu full of delicious dishes, to our attentive service staff, it is easy to understand how Chop Life has been so successful in recreating the true flavor of Africa for all its guests. Whether you are a seasoned eater or a novice explorer of African food, Chop Life gives you an incomparable experience that is sure to please even the most discerning palate.
